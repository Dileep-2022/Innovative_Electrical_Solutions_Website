Our Products

We specialize in creating customizable control panels tailored to meet specific needs and preferences.
Energy Meter Panel

An Energy Meter Panel is a dashboard interface that provides real-time monitoring and analysis of electrical energy consumption and performance metrics. It helps in tracking usage, detecting inefficiencies, and optimizing energy management.

Main Distibution Panel

A Main Distribution Panel (MDP) is a central hub in an electrical system that distributes power from a main source to various circuits within a building or facility. It includes protective devices and controls to manage and safeguard electrical distribution.

MCC Panel

A Motor Control Center (MCC) Panel is an assembly of one or more enclosed sections containing motor control units, including motor starters, contactors, overload relays, and other components used to control and protect electric motors. It centralizes control and monitoring functions for industrial motor-driven equipment.

Power Distubution Panel

A Power Distribution Panel is a critical component in electrical systems that receives power from a primary source and distributes it to various circuits or loads within a building or facility. It typically includes circuit breakers or fuses for overcurrent protection and can accommodate multiple branches of electrical distribution.

Change Over Panel

A Changeover Panel, also known as an Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS), is a device used to automatically transfer power supply between two sources (such as mains and a backup generator) in case of a power outage or when one source fails. It ensures uninterrupted power supply to critical loads.

AMF Panel

An AMF Panel (Auto Mains Failure Panel) is an essential component in electrical systems that automatically switches the power source from mains electricity to a backup generator in the event of a mains power failure. It ensures continuous power supply to critical equipment or systems without manual intervention.

Agricultural Starter

A Motor Starter used in agriculture is an electrical device designed to start and stop electric motors used in farming equipment such as irrigation pumps, conveyor belts, and agricultural machinery.

Feeder Piller Box

A Feeder Pillar Box is an outdoor electrical enclosure used to house electrical components such as circuit breakers, fuses, contactors, and sometimes transformers. It serves as a distribution point where electrical power is fed into various circuits or loads within a facility or outdoor area, providing protection and accessibility for maintenance and operation purposes.

DTC Panel

A DTC Panel typically stands for "Distribution Transformer Center Panel." It is a type of electrical panel that houses equipment related to the distribution and control of electricity from a distribution transformer. This includes circuit breakers, fuses, meters, and other components necessary for managing electrical distribution within a specific area or facility.
